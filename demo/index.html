About Us

Company Overview

Welcome to Aqua Rinse! Established in 2024, Aqua Rinse has quickly become a cornerstone of quality laundry services in Udupi and its surrounding areas. With a steadfast commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction, Aqua Rinse has garnered a reputation for delivering impeccable results and exceptional service. At Aqua Rinse, we understand the importance of clean laundry to our customers' daily lives.

Whether it's freshening up your wardrobe, ensuring crisp linens for your guests, or handling bulky items like comforters and blankets, we take pride in providing a comprehensive range of laundry solutions to meet every need. Our state-of-the-art facilities are equipped with advanced machinery and environmentally friendly practices to ensure the highest standards of cleanliness while minimizing our ecological footprint. From sorting to washing, drying, and folding, every step of our process is meticulously executed to deliver impeccable results.

Our Mission

Our mission revolves around delivering seamless laundry solutions, placing utmost importance on convenience, reliability, and exceptional quality. We understand the demands of modern life, which is why we've designed user-friendly platforms and optimized processes to streamline laundry care. By prioritizing hassle-free experiences, we aim to save our customers valuable time and effort while ensuring accessibility to laundry services.
In tandem with convenience, our commitment to reliability is unwavering. We hold ourselves to the highest standards of consistency, punctuality, and attention to detail. From the moment of pickup to the final delivery, our customers can trust in a dependable experience every step of the way. This dedication to reliability is underscored by our use of top-quality materials and skilled professionals, guaranteeing thorough cleaning and maintaining garment integrity. With these principles at the forefront, we strive to not only meet but exceed our customers' expectations, building lasting relationships founded on trust and satisfaction.


Service Offering

We offer a range of laundry services to meet your needs, including:

  • Regular laundry: Trust us with your everyday laundry needs, from clothing to household linens. We'll ensure your garments are clean, fresh, and ready to wear.
  • Specialty items (e.g., linens, uniforms): Whether it's hotel linens, restaurant uniforms, or corporate attire, we specialize in cleaning specialty items to maintain their cleanliness and professionalism.
  • Dry cleaning: For garments that require dry cleaning, we utilize advanced techniques and eco-friendly solvents to ensure impeccable results without compromising on fabric quality.
  • Alterations and repairs: In addition to cleaning, we offer alteration and repair services to keep your clothing in top condition. From hemming to patching, our skilled seamstresses will ensure your garments fit and look their best.

Our pickup and delivery options ensure a hassle-free experience for our customers, with flexible scheduling and quick turnaround times.
